Frequently Asked Questions

What if no photographer accepts my request?

If your request is not accepted within 1 hour, you will be automatically fully refunded. We pride ourselves on fast matching!

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before your scheduled time. After that, no refunds are provided.

What happens if it rains?

We don't cancel for rain! Tokyo looks incredibly cinematic in the rain. Grab a clear umbrella (available at any convenience store) and let's shoot!

What if I'm late?
